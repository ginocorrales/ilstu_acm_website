ACM at Illinois State

The ACM at Illinois State University is the local student chapter of the Association for Computing Machinery.

Group meeting announcments are made on our ACM Facebook page, as well as the joint ACM and AITP IT Club Facebook group.

SIGs

We have Special Interest Groups that focus on a variety of subjects, including game development, web, graphics, mobile, and business systems.

The SIGs meet during ACM meetings as well as have their own separate meetings.
