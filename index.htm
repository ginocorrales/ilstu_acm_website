ACM at Illinois State

The ACM at Illinois State University is the local student chapter of the Association for Computing Machinery.

SIGs

We have Special Interest Groups that focus on a variety of subjects, including game development, web, graphics, mobile, and enterprise.

Recruiters

We work with recruiters to help match IT students with skills that employers are looking for.
